Welcome to HH ("the App"), a free mobile application that helps users discover happy hour deals across Dallas, Texas. This Privacy Policy explains how Find HH ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the App.
By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
2. Information We Collect
a. Information You Provide
•	Email address — collected when you create an account
•	Password — stored in encrypted form; we never store plain-text passwords

b. Information Collected Automatically
•	Device information (device type, operating system version)
•	App usage data (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
•	Crash reports and diagnostic data

If you grant permission, the App accesses your device's location to show you nearby venues and sort results by distance. Location data is used only within the App and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at portervenue@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
